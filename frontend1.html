Restaurant Information Desk

Welcome to our project four DB, the application called Restaurant Information Desk. This application will allow you to store restaurants, and their menu items.

To the left side of this applicaiton, you will be able to see application operations related to storing restaurants and their food menus. Please fill in the text fields accordingly and click the button that corresponds to each section accordingly to fulfill the desired app operation.

To right side of this application, you will be able to do application operations related to establishing a map based on pre-existing restaurants (inserted beforehand from the left side of this application). For instance, you can establish paths between restaurants with a specific distance and return the shortest path from one restaurant to the other.
